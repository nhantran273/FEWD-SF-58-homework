About Me

I was born in Sacramento, Ca. on January 19, 1975. Moved to Clovis, Ca.(Next to Fresno) in 1983. I have been raised in the country for the first 22 years of my life. I grew up with Dogs, Horses and Dirtbikes. I was taught to drive heavy equipment since I was 12. I would help my family plow snow in the Sierra Nevada's. It was very long hours and little pay since I was working for the family.

In 1998 I was offered a job to work for one of the telecom giants. Upon accepting the offer, it did require that I move to the Bay Area. Fastforwarding to 2005. I started my own business as a licensed vehicle registration service.

As for my personal life, I have always been a very active and adventerous individual. Started snowboarding since the age of 16. I have been to almost every resort in California. Even the crappy ones. My true passions other than this coding class is Jiu Jitsu and CrossCountry Mountain Biking.

I have 2 beautiful kids. My son is 9 and my daughter is 6. They are my first priority in life other than taking this class.
